About Us

Quoded is built to bring control of your professional and personal lives in the palm of your hand. Utilizing the ubiquitous instant messaging interface, Quoded has integrated productivity, meetings and task-management into one mobile application that can be adopted for personal and professional use without silos.

We recognize that communication is about inclusivity and removing barriers. In the story of productivity, chat is the main character. We have therefore, put conversations first and task-management next. Using our application you can bring harmony between your personal and professional lives. Find people easily, discuss ideas, assign tasks and follow-up on them, all from one chat window.
